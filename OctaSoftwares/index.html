NFC : What is it ?

Near Field Communication (NFC) is a collection of short-range wireless technologies that generally require a distance of 4 cm or less to establish a connection. Between an NFC tag and an Android-powered smartphone, or between two Android-powered devices, NFC enables the sharing of tiny data payloads.
